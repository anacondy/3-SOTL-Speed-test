## About

Silence of the Lags is a modern, elegant internet speed testing application designed to provide accurate measurements across all devices and platforms. The application features:

- Real-time download and upload speed measurement
- Network latency (ping) and jitter analysis
- Packet loss detection
- Beautiful animated visualizations
- Cross-platform support (Web, Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, Linux)
